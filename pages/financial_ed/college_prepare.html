Preparing for College

V & V Advisors is dedicated to empowering families with clear, trustworthy strategies for saving, applying for financial aid, and making informed decisions that keep higher education affordable.

Start Early: Building a College Savings Plan

The sooner you start saving, the more time your money has to grow through compounding. Explore options like 529 College Savings Plans and Coverdell Education Savings Accounts.

Plan Type Key Features Tax Advantage
529 College Savings Plan Funds grow tax-free when used for qualified education expenses Tax-free growth and withdrawals
Coverdell ESA Can be used for K–12 and college expenses Tax-free withdrawals for education

Source: U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC)

Completing the Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) is essential for accessing grants, scholarships, and low-interest federal loans. Apply early — many states and schools have priority deadlines.

You can estimate your eligibility with the Federal Student Aid Estimator.
