Our Values & Code of Ethics

At V & V Advisors, we are dedicated to empowering families with the knowledge and tools for financial literacy, protection, and wealth-building strategies through healthy financial habits.

Our Core Values

These guiding values shape every service, conversation, and decision we make — ensuring we serve our clients and communities with the highest integrity and care.

Integrity

We uphold honesty, fairness, and transparency in all financial guidance and interactions.

We provide fact-based advice backed by reputable sources such as CFPB and FDIC.

Education

We believe financial literacy is the foundation for empowerment and generational stability.

We simplify complex topics — from budgeting to insurance — and reference trusted educational resources like FINRA Investor Education.

Trust

Trust is earned through consistency and accountability.

We align our actions with promises, ensuring families can rely on our advice and transparency.

Empowerment

We help individuals and families gain control of their financial future.

We offer tools and coaching to help people set goals, save, and invest with confidence.

Respect

Every client’s story, background, and financial journey are valued and honored.

We provide an inclusive, judgment-free environment where every family feels supported.

Our Code of Ethics

The V & V Advisors Code of Ethics outlines the moral compass that guides our professional behavior and protects our clients’ trust.

  • Client-First Commitment: Always act in the best interest of the families we serve.
  • Honesty in Communication: Present all information truthfully, free of misleading statements or omissions.
  • Confidentiality: Safeguard personal and financial information with utmost care.
  • Transparency in Fees and Partnerships: Clearly disclose all compensation and affiliations.
  • Continuous Learning: Commit to staying informed about financial laws, consumer rights, and ethical standards.
  • Compliance with Regulations: Operate in full accordance with state and federal standards such as those set by the
  • California Department of Insurance (CDI)
  • NAIC

Together, We Build Stronger Financial Futures

These values and ethical principles aren’t just words — they guide how we help every family grow with clarity and confidence.
